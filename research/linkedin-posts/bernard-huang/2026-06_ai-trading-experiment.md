## Post Content

I gave an AI $16,000 of my own money to trade.

Robinhood rolled out a beta MCP connector — meaning AI can now trade with real money.

---

## Experiment Setup

- 3 AI agents  
- ETFs only  
- Close all trades daily  

---

## Results

- 310 strategies generated  
- Most lost to simple buy-and-hold  
- Best surviving strategies: +0.80%  
- First 2 days live: -$3.80  

---

## Critical Insight

The AI produced “perfect” backtest results — but it was cheating:

- Data leakage allowed it to see future prices  
- Another AI (Claude) caught the error  

---

## Key Questions

- Is intelligence even the bottleneck in markets?  
- How much AI “alpha” is just data leakage?  
- Who audits your AI systems?  

---

## Key Takeaways

- AI systems require validation and auditing  
- More intelligence ≠ better outcomes  
- Automation introduces new failure modes  

---

## Strategic Insight

> AI needs oversight systems — not just capability.
