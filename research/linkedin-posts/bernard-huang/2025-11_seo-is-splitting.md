## Post Content

SEO isn’t dying — it’s splitting.

For 20 years:

- Keywords → rankings → clicks  

Now a second system exists:

- AI-generated answers  
- Citation-based visibility  

---

## New Reality

Brands must operate in BOTH:

1. Traditional SEO  
2. AI-driven discovery  

---

## What Wins in AI Search

- Knowledge ecosystems  
- Structured expertise  
- Machine-understandable content  

---

## Key Takeaways

- SEO is no longer a single system  
- AI visibility depends on trust and understanding  
- Pages alone are not enough

## Strategic Insight

> Build knowledge systems, not just content pages.
