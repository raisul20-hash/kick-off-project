# Query Deserves a Page (QDP)

**Author:** Koray Tuğberk GÜBÜR  
**Platform:** LinkedIn  
**Date:** February 25, 2026  
**Source:** https://tr.linkedin.com/in/koray-tugberk-gubur  

---

## Concept

Every meaningful query should map to:

➡️ A **dedicated page**

---

## Core Idea

- Queries represent **distinct intents**
- Each intent deserves:
  - Focused content
  - Specific relevance
  - Clear entity coverage  

---

## Implementation

- Build pages based on:
  - Query clusters  
  - Intent segmentation  
  - Semantic relationships  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Avoid:
  - Overloading pages with multiple intents  
- Focus on:
  - Precision
  - Relevance
  - Coverage  

---

## Strategic Insight

> Proper query-to-page mapping improves indexing, ranking, and user satisfaction
