## Post Content

SEO is SO easy now. You just need to:

- Find, qualify, and prioritize keywords  
- Identify topic, backlinks, and brand awareness gaps  
- Fix technical / UX issues  
- Create pages that match intent, are built for both users and agents, and continually iterate  
- Build topic authority across your website and owned assets (e.g., YouTube)  
- Understand how search works across:
  - Traditional search
  - AI platforms
  - Social
  - Ecommerce
  - App stores  
- Perform outreach and secure earned media opportunities  
- Audit and maintain brand reputation and entity consistency  
- Allocate labor and capital effectively to generate ROI  
- Run ongoing tests based on real-world results (not theory)  
- Build and maintain agentic workflows and systems  
- Stay on top of rapid daily changes in search, AI, and agents  

> But you're right: Claude will just do it all for you and you don't have to think at all 😉  

---

## Key Takeaways

- SEO complexity has **increased significantly**, not decreased  
- AI does **not replace strategic thinking**  
- Modern SEO = **Search Everywhere Optimization**  
- Execution requires multi-channel + technical + strategic alignment  

---

## Notes

This post is clearly sarcastic, highlighting how **SEO has become more complex in the AI era**, contrary to claims that AI tools make it “easy.”
