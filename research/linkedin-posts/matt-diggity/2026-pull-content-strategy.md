# Shift from Push to Pull Content Marketing in 2026

Source: Matt Diggity LinkedIn

Key ideas:
- Push content (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook) has a short lifespan.
- Pull content (YouTube, blogs, Pinterest) compounds over time.
- Evergreen search-based content generates leads for years.
- Vanity metrics like followers and likes are less important than revenue attribution.
- Email lists remain one of the most valuable owned assets.

Main recommendations:
1. Focus on search-driven content platforms.
2. Publish fewer but deeper pieces of content.
3. Use UTM tracking to measure revenue impact.
4. Build an email list from the start.
