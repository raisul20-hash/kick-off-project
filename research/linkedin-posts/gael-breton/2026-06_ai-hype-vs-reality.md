# AI Hype vs Reality in Marketing

**Author:** Gael Breton  
**Platform:** LinkedIn (derived from recent discussions & shares)  
**Date:** June 2026  

---

## Post Content

Everyone is talking about AI like it will replace entire marketing teams overnight.

It won’t.

Most of what you see:
- Overhyped tools
- Shallow workflows
- Demo-level results

AI is not magic.

It’s leverage.

---

## What Actually Works

- Automating **repeatable workflows**
- Using AI as:
  - A research assistant
  - A drafting tool
  - A system connector

> Think of AI as a "calculator for text", not a replacement for thinking.

---

## What Doesn’t Work

- Fully automated SEO campaigns  
- One-click content generation  
- “Agents will run your business” narratives  

---

## Key Takeaways

- AI amplifies skill — it doesn’t replace it  
- Most marketers are still using AI incorrectly  
- Competitive advantage = **systems + execution**

---

## Strategic Insight

> Stop chatting with AI. Start delegating structured work to it.
