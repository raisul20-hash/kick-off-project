## The Key Difference

Bad AI usage:
- Prompt → output → done  

Good AI usage:
- System → workflow → iteration  

---

## Core Principle

AI should:

- Integrate with your tools  
- Persist across workflows  
- Reduce manual repetition  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Workflows beat prompts  
- Systems beat hacks  
- Integration beats isolation
