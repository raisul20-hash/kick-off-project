# GEO vs SEO Requires Different Metrics

**Author:** Aleyda Solis  
**Platform:** LinkedIn  
**Date:** ~May–June 2026  
**Source:** https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:ugcPost:7430295105351311360/  

---

## Post Content

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) won’t replace SEO.

It will build on it.

But marketers must define different:

- Goals  
- Metrics  
- Outcomes  

For each channel.

---

## Key Differences

| Area        | SEO                     | GEO (AI Search)              |
|------------|------------------------|------------------------------|
| Goal       | Rankings + traffic     | Citations + visibility       |
| Metrics    | Clicks, positions      | Mentions, inclusion in answers |
| Output     | SERP presence          | AI-generated responses       |

---

## Key Takeaways

- GEO is **not identical to SEO**  
- Measurement frameworks must evolve  
- Visibility in AI ≠ rankings  

---

## Strategic Insight

> Treat AI search as a separate channel with its own KPIs.
