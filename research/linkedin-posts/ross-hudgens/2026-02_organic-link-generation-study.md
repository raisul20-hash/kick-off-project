## Post Content

We analyzed which blogs generate the most organic links.

---

### Key Finding

Some industries naturally generate links at scale:

- Data-rich industries (e.g., real estate like Zillow)  
- Technical/compliance niches (e.g., SOC2 tools)  

Others require:

- Digital PR  
- Manual outreach  

---

### Core Strategy

Tie:

- Data  
- Content  
- Search demand  

→ This creates a **compounding link "snowball"**

---

### Key Insight

The best content:

- Starts with promotion  
- Then scales organically  

---

### Takeaways

- Data-driven content earns passive links  
- Some niches have built-in link advantages  
- Outreach helps ignite growth but isn’t the end goal  

---

## Strategic Insight

> The goal is not just links — it's building assets that generate links continuously.
