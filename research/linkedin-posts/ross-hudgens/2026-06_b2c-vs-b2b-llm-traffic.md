# B2C vs B2B Content Performance in LLM Traffic

**Date:** June 18, 2026

---

## Post Content

B2C content marketing does not drive many direct LLM clicks in 2026.

In our analysis of 61 brands:

- B2C editorial content → only ~7% of LLM traffic  
- B2B editorial content → 25%+ of clicks  

---

### Why?

In B2B:

- BOFU content exists:
  - “Best X”
  - “Alternatives”
- These pages:
  - Get cited more
  - Drive LLM clicks  

In B2C:

- These queries are dominated by:
  - Affiliate sites  
  - Publishers  

---

### Strategy for B2C

To win visibility:

- Optimize product pages  
- Increase domain authority  
- Get included in affiliate lists  

---

### Key Takeaways

- LLM traffic behaves differently by vertical  
- B2B benefits from content marketing more than B2C  
- B2C must focus on **product + distribution ecosystems**

## Strategic Insight

> SEO/GEO strategy must adapt based on business model — not all content performs equally in AI systems.
