# AI + SEO = Systems, Not Shortcuts

**Author:** Mark Webster  
**Platform:** LinkedIn + Authority Hacker insights  
**Date:** 2025–2026  

---

## Core Idea

AI doesn’t replace SEO.

It enhances systems.

---

## Where AI Works

- Workflow automation  
- Data processing  
- Content drafting  
- Outreach systems  

---

## Where AI Fails

- Strategy  
- Differentiation  
- Original thinking  

---

## Key Takeaways

- AI is a multiplier, not a replacement  
- Systems create leverage  
- Execution quality still matters  

---

## Strategic Insight

> AI makes good systems better — and bad systems faster.
