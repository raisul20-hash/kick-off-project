# The Problem With Link Metrics

**Author:** Mark Webster  
**Platform:** LinkedIn (derived from post insights)  
**Date:** 2025  

---

## Core Idea

Most SEO teams optimize for the wrong metrics.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Tracking:
  - Domain Rating (DR)  
  - Traffic  
  - Link volume  

Instead of:

- Actual ranking impact  

---

## What Happens

- Teams hit KPIs  
- Rankings don’t improve  
- Budget gets wasted  

---

## Better Approach

Focus on:

- Link relevance  
- Editorial placement  
- Authority transfer  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Not all links are equal  
- Metrics can be misleading  
- Impact matters more than volume  

---

## Strategic Insight

> If rankings don’t move, the links don’t matter.
