# Step 3: Build a Topic Domination Plan

Start with a seed keyword that has **investigative intent**.

Example:

```text
Best AEO tools for agencies
```

---

## Expand Your Topic Map

### Existing Websites

1. Open Google Search Console.
2. Filter by the seed topic.
3. Export the query data.
4. Use AI to generate supporting topics.
5. Organize everything in a spreadsheet.

### New Websites

Use topic clustering tools to automatically generate related topics.

### Minimum Recommendation

Aim for:

* **100+ unique supporting topics**

---

```text
Seed Topic

├── Supporting Topic 1
├── Supporting Topic 2
├── Supporting Topic 3
├── ...
└── Supporting Topic 100+
```

# Step 4: Create SEO Content AI Loves

Content should be built using a **knowledge base**.

## Build SEO Super Intelligence

Collect:

* Products and services
* Offers
* FAQs
* Testimonials
* Social proof
* Brand voice

Convert these into structured artifacts.

Upload those artifacts into an AI workspace.

* ChatGPT Projects

* Understand your brand better
* Produce more personalized content
* Maintain consistency

### Possibilities

* Additional insights

## Analyze Competitors

# Treat Content Like a Product

Never publish the first draft.

Content should go through multiple iterations before going live.

```text
Research
    ↓
Draft
    ↓
Edit
    ↓
Improve
    ↓
Publish
```

# Build Supporting Assets

After publishing the primary page:

Create at least **9 supporting pages**.

```text
Primary Topic
│
├── Supporting Asset 1
├── Supporting Asset 2
├── Supporting Asset 3
├── ...
└── Supporting Asset 9
```

# Step 5: Attack the Same Topics on Owned Assets

Don't rely solely on your website.

---

## Check Indexability

Paste your social profile URL into Google.

If recent posts appear:

✅ Use that platform.

Otherwise:

❌ Move on.

---

# Why YouTube Should Be Next

YouTube is powerful because:

1. It is the second-largest search engine.
2. Videos rank in Google.
3. AI systems frequently cite YouTube.

---

# Repurpose Website Topics Into Videos

Don't read articles word-for-word.

Instead:

Transform articles into video scripts.

---

# Build a YouTube Funnel

Never publish random videos.

Start with a conversion asset.

Example:

```text
Rankability Review
```

This video should:

* Demonstrate the product
* Lead users to a landing page
* Drive conversions

---

## Supporting Videos

Example:

```text
Best AEO Tools for Agencies
```

The CTA should direct viewers to:

```text
Rankability Review
```

---

# Funnel Structure

```text
Awareness Videos
       ↓
Comparison Videos
       ↓
Review Videos
       ↓
Conversion Asset
       ↓
Landing Page
```

---

## Benefits

* Builds trust faster.
* Improves watch time.
* Increases session duration.
* Builds topic authority.

# Step 6: Control Your Brand Narrative

AI only knows what exists on the internet.

Therefore:

You must control the information available about your brand.

---

## Create a Super FAQ

Answer every branded question.

Examples:

```text
Does Rankability have an API?

How much does Rankability cost?

Who founded Rankability?
```

---

## Why This Matters

If you don't answer these questions:

AI will search elsewhere.

That information may be:

* Inaccurate
* Outdated
* Misleading

First-party sources are preferred.
